About the DMV Boat Owners Association

The DMVBOA is a growing community of boat owners and outdoor enthusiasts dedicated to safe boating, adventure, and local events across the DC, Maryland, and Virginia region.

Our Mission

To connect boaters of all experience levels through social events, training, and partnerships that promote safety and enjoyment on the water.

Who We Are

Founded by passionate boaters, the DMVBOA provides opportunities for networking, skill-building, and member recognition throughout the year.
