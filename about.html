About DMVBOA

Building community on and off the water

Who We Are

The DMV Boat Owners Association (DMVBOA) is a community-driven organization created to bring together boat owners and boating enthusiasts across Washington DC, Maryland, and Virginia.

Our goal is to foster connection, education, and shared experiences through on-water adventures, social events, and boating safety initiatives.

Our Mission

DMVBOA exists to build a welcoming boating community that promotes safe boating practices, meaningful connections, and memorable experiences for boaters of all backgrounds and experience levels.

What We Do

  • Organize on-water and land-based boating events
  • Provide boating education and safety resources
  • Support local marinas and marine businesses
  • Encourage responsible and enjoyable boating
