Membership Benefits

Join the DMV Boat Owners Association and enjoy exclusive benefits designed to make your boating experience more enjoyable, affordable, and connected.

  • 30% discount on covered slips at Holly Acres Marine (first year)
  • Free and discounted boating and safety training sessions
  • Discounted membership with Freedom Boat Club Northern VA
  • Member savings on Argo Navigation app
  • Access to exclusive events, raffles, and giveaways

Why Join?

DMVBOA members are part of a growing network of boaters who share knowledge, resources, and opportunities to explore the waters of the DMV area together. Whether you’re new to boating or a seasoned captain, there’s a place for you here.
